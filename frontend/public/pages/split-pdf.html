Frequently Asked Questions

Enter the page numbers in the Page Range field separated by commas (e.g., 1,3,5). Ranges like 2-6 are also supported. Leave it empty to extract all pages.

If you split into more than one page, you'll receive a ZIP archive containing one PDF per extracted page. Single-page extractions download as a standalone PDF.

There is no page limit per se, but very large PDFs (1000+ pages) may take longer to process. We recommend files under 200 MB for best performance.
